---
title: "The Lean Context Thesis"
description: "How Humans and AI Agents Make Each Other Smarter"
---

Untargeted upfront context makes AI agents worse, not better. The fix is not better prompts — it is a better system.

The Lean Context Thesis proposes a structural approach to human-agent collaboration: a three-tier architecture maintained by disciplined compaction and governed by a cultural contract between the human and the agent.
